SOUTH UNITED SPORTS SCHOOLS

South United Sports Schools is a multi-city network of football training academies in India. We also run development and community programmes in various cities under the aegis of South United Football Club. Apart from football coaching and training, we also conduct Summer Camps and Sports Clinics across the country.

Our training programmes begin from the tender age of 3, upwards to 21 and above. We classify them as The South United Sporty Tots Programme (3-5 years), South United Sports Schools (6-20 years), and South United Open Age training (21 and above).

If you are a parent or school interested in enrolling your children in a programme for quality football training, kindly enter your information on the Contact Page.

SPORTY TOTS PROGRAMME

The Sporty Tots Programme is a unique way of introducing children to the world of sport and fitness through fun and interactive sessions conducted by qualified and professional coaches.

Between the ages of 3 and 6, it is essential that children have fun while playing the sport, as well as gain basic social and motor skills. We believe Sport is the optimal way to inculcate such competencies in a child and a great way to induce self-confidence and leadership in the long run.

The programme includes regular monitoring and evaluation of students based on attendance, growth, and development.
