CLUB HISTORY

In 2012, when South United Football Club was first established, we competed in the I-League Second Division in that very year. We now find ourselves preparing for competition at the same level having rung a host of changes to the side since then. We have consistently performed at the highest levels of football in Karnataka. We’ve achieved this through focus on youth and development of local talent so far.

Our goal with South United Football Club is to actively contribute to the Indian Footballing movement that enables the youth of our nation to consider careers in the game a viable option. We’re determined to be a part of the game at multiple levels to achieve this, from the setting up of an academy, implementation of Soccer Schools, to reaching out to underprivileged children through the Foundation, while playing football competitively.
